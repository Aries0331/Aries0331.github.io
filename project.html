Crux AR

Crux AR is an enterprise web-app that helps businesses track and increase engagement
with their documents to convert into better sales.
It’s a PDF content management, tracking and presentation solution built for selling in the 21st century.
It will be used to enable better Business to business sales through tracking which parts of proposals are most effective.
Work done in Fall 2017. Visit our website at CruxAR.

Beep

Beep is an Android application implmented in Fall 2016 with a group of five.
It is an application that calls or offers for rides.
The user can be a driver that offers ride by searching keywords, location or even price range.
A user can be a rider as well who asks for a ride by entering location.
Check our video demo on Youtube.
