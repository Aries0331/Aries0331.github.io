Beep

Beep is an Android application implmented in Fall 2016 with a group of five.
It is a calling and offering for drive application.
The user can be a driver that offers ride by searching keywords or location.
A user can be a rider as well who asks for a ride by entering location.
Check our video demo on Youtube.
